BHINDI FRY

Hi, I'm Khushi
I'm glad you are here, thankyou for joining me!

Ingredients

  • Bhindi
  • Powder : red chilli, haldi, salt, black pepper, garam masala, aamchur (you can add other as well)
  • Oil for frying
  • Besan
  • Rice Flour

Steps

  1. Wash your bhindi properly and dry them with help of cotton cloth
  2. Chop head and tail of bhindi
  3. Now slit them in betweern, if you do not like big peices you can cut them in two as well.
  4. In the meantime, add all the malasa mentioned in a bowl and mix them.
  5. put this hand-made masala into the cut of bhindi
  6. coat them first by rice flour and then with besan
  7. In the oil, fry them for 15 minutes or more if they are not cooked properly
  8. Enjoy
