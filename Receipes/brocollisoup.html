BROCCOLI SOUP

Hi, I'm Khushi

Ingredients

  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup milk (or dairy-free alternative)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated cheese for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and minced garlic to the pot. Cook until onions are translucent, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add broccoli florets to the pot and cook for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Pour in vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until broccoli is tender.
  6. Using an immersion blender, blend the soup until smooth. Alternatively, transfer the soup to a blender in batches and blend until smooth. Be cautious as the soup will be hot.
  7. Return the blended soup to the pot over low heat.
  8. Stir in milk and season with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes.
  9. Remove from heat and ladle the soup into bowls.
  10. Garnish with grated cheese if desired.
  11. Serve hot and enjoy!
