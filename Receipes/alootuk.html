ALOO TUK

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Powder : red chilli, haldi, salt, black pepper, garam masala, aamchur (you can add other as well)
  • Oil for frying
  • Lemon
  • corainder

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them in 1-2 inch thick slices
  2. transfer the potatoes in a pan filled with water.
  3. now take slices out and wrap them in cotton cloth and rub them.
  4. Add oil in one pan and keep it on gas, after oil is hot, carefully add slices in the oil.
  5. Let the potatoes fry for 10-15 minutes or till there's a thin layer of red-golden layer covering potato slices
  6. Remove potatoes and then press them down with the help of glass.
  7. In the same oil, again add crushed potatoes in oil and fry till golden-brown.
  8. In the meantime, add all the malasa mentioned in a bowl and mix them.
  9. After potatoes are fried, sprinkle masala on top of it and mix with potatoes.
  10. Add lemon water according to your taste and add bit of corainder
  11. Enjoy
