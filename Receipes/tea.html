TEA

Ingredients
  • Milk
  • Tea powder
  • Sugar
  • Grated ginger
  • Elachi
  • Cloves
  • Mint leaves
  • Tulsi leaves
Directions
  1. In a pan, add 2 tablespoon of sugar and let it caramalise it.
  2. Then add half a cup of water and let it boil for 3 boils
  3. Then add 2 tablespoon of tea leaves and watch the color changing
  4. When the colour changes to reddish sort of add all the powders mentioned and let it boil
  5. When water is half, add Milk in to pan
  6. let the tea boil for 9-10 boils
  7. Strain into the cup through a strainer
  8. Enjoy
