Masala Khichdi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1/2 cup split yellow moong dal (lentils)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit lengthwise
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter) or oil
  • 4 cups water
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Wash the rice and moong dal together under running water until the water runs clear. Soak them in water for about 20 minutes. Then, drain the water and set aside.
  2. In a pressure cooker or a deep pot, heat ghee or oil over medium heat.
  3. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  5. Now, add grated ginger, minced garlic, and slit green chilies. Sauté for another couple of minutes.
  6. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.
  7. Once the tomatoes are cooked, add turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well and cook for a minute.
  8. Now, add the drained rice and moong dal to the cooker or pot. Mix everything together.
  9. Pour in the water and stir well. Adjust salt if needed.
  10. Close the pressure cooker lid and cook for 3 whistles or if using a pot, cover with a lid and cook until the rice and dal are soft and fully cooked.
  11. Once done, let the pressure release naturally before opening the cooker lid.
  12. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves and serve hot.
