HONEY AND COFFEE GALAO

Hi, I'm Khushi
I'm glad you are here, thankyou for joining me!

Ingredients

  • Coffee
  • Honey
  • Milk
  • Vanilla essence

Directions

  1. In your mug, add honey and coffee.
  2. Mix them and add 1-2 table-spoon of warm water.
  3. In a seperate pan, add milk and let it simmer, not too hot.
  4. While milk is on stove add vanilla essence, beat the honey and coffee mixture till foam is formed.
  5. Make sure milk is not too hot, transfer the milk in the cup.
  6. Mix it well taste it and if u want you can add more honey or even sugar..
  7. Enjoy
