ORANGE TEA

Hi, I'm Khushi
I'm glad you are here, thankyou for joining me!

Ingredients

  • One orange
  • One tea bag (of your choice)
  • 250 ml water

Steps

  1. Add 250 ml water in pan & let it simmer for 2 minutes.
  2. After 3 boils, transfer the water in a mug with a tea bag in it.
  3. In the same pan, add orange pulp with 20ml of water and mash the pulp.
  4. with the lowest flame, let this mixture come to one boil.
  5. Transfer this mixture into the mug bt straining it.
  6. Dip the tea bag 4-5 well so tea is infused with orange water.
  7. Enjoy
