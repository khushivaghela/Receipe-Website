DATES CHEESECAKE

Hi, I'm Khushi
I'm glad you are here, thankyou for joining me!

Ingredients

  • 15 pitted dates
  • 1 banana
  • 2 tbspn choco powder
  • 2 tbspn coco powder
  • 10 marie-gold biscuit
  • 2 tbspn butter

Steps

  1. In a blender add marie-gold biscuit and a make a rough mixture
  2. In a cake mould add the mixture with butter, mix it and press it gently
  3. Let it freeze for 30 minutes to 1 hour
  4. In the same mixture add the other ingredients and a make a smooth paste
  5. If dates pudding is too sweet for you, add some coffee in it.
  6. If the mixture is too thick add a bit of water and run blender again.
  7. Make sure pudding isn't runny it should be thick but not too much
  8. In the cake mould add this mixture
  9. you can keep it in freezer as it is or you can freeze it for thirty minutes or till better is set and than add layer of nutella which is completely optional.
  10. After adding a layer let it freeze for 8 hours or overnight
  11. If you have not added nutella that's completely fine and you also have to keep it in freezer for 8 hours
  12. After taking out of freeze, cut it in pieces like cake.
  13. Enjoy
