Tomato Soup Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 large tomatoes, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and minced garlic to the pot. Saut√© until onions are translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add diced tomatoes, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to the pot. Cook for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Pour in vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil. Once boiling, reduce heat to low and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Remove the pot from heat and allow it to cool slightly.
  6. Using an immersion blender or regular blender, puree the soup until smooth.
  7. Return the pureed soup to the pot and stir in heavy cream if using. Heat the soup over low heat until warmed through.
  8. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary.
  9. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves if desired.
