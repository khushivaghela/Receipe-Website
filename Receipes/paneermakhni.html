PANEER MAKHNA

Hi, I'm Khushi
I'm glad you are here, thankyou for joining me!

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 tomato, sliced
  • 2 tbsp cashew
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 3 pod cardamom
  • 3 cloves
  • 1 inch cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp butter
  • 2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 15 cubes paneer
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 chilli, slit
  • 1 inch ginger, chopped
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • ½ tsp coriander powder
  • ¼ tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp cream
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi, crushed

Steps

  1. firstly, to prepare onion tomato base, in a pan take 1 onion, 3 tomato and 2 tbsp cashew.
  2. add 1 bay leaf, 3 pods cardamom, 3 cloves, 1 inch cinnamon and 1 tsp ginger garlic paste.
  3. also, add 1 tsp butter and 2 cup water.
  4. mix well, cover and boil for 10 minutes or until everything are cooked well.
  5. remove the bay leaf and cool completely before grinding to smooth paste. keep aside.
  6. in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp butter, 2 tbsp oil and fry 15 cubes paneer.
  7. once the paneer turns light golden, keep aside and in the same oil, add 1 tsp cumin, 1 chilli and 1 inch ginger. saute on low flame until the spices turn aromatic.
  8. keeping the flame on low, add ¼ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chilli powder, ½ tsp coriander powder and ¼ tsp garam masala and saute on low flame until the spices turn aromatic.
  9. pour in a prepared onion tomato base and mix well. keep mixing until the oil separates. also, add 2 tbsp cream and ¾ tsp salt. mix well.
  10. cover and boil for 5 minutes or until the base is cooked well.
  11. add in fried paneer, 1 tsp kasuri methi and 2 tbsp coriander. mix well.
  12. you can serve it with rice or roti
  13. Enjoy
