Hello, I am Khushi Vaghela, owner & creator of this receipe website. My passion for cooking made a chef and this passion has been in me since I was kid. The purpose of this website is to share easy and healthy receipes to everyone with the most basic ingredients.
Whether you're a seasoned chef or a kitchen novice, our user-friendly recipes cater to all skill levels. We provide step-by-step instructions, accompanied by stunning visuals, ensuring that your cooking experience is not just delicious but also visually captivating. So, tie on your apron, sharpen those knives, and get ready to your inner hidden chef.

The reason for creating such a website is to share receipes that I make for me and my family, in a world where everyone is scrolling reels and seeing receipes cooked under a minute, i want to share how much time and effort it really does and more importantly how healthy not just for you but to your family and our planet.
My receipes are pure vegetarian and many of them are swaminarayan, jain & vegan friendly as well.
