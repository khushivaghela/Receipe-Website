About the dish

It's a savory and flavorful chaat usually served warm/hot, which also happens to be a very popular street food of india.

Ingredients
  • sweet potatoes - it's upto you
  • chat masala - a pinch
  • lemon - half
  • oil/butter - 1-2 tbspn.
Directions
  1. Turn on gas & keep a pan on it.
  2. While the gas is on low flame, wash sweet potatoes & dry it with cotton cloth.
  3. Grease the pan & sweet potatoes with oil/butter
  4. Once the pan is hot, place the sweet potatoes on it & cover it with a lid.
  5. After 4-5 minutes, open the lid.
  6. Try to insert a fork in the part where it was being cooked.
  7. If it goes easily, turn the potato or else continue cooking on the same part and make sure to cover it with the lid.
  8. Repeat the same process till sweet potatoes are boiled perfectly.
  9. After being boiled perfectly from all sides and turn the gas off.
  10. Once it cools down abit, start removing the skin.
  11. Cut the boiled Sweet potatoes into small pieces.
  12. You can eat it as it is or can add chat masala & squeeze 4-5 drops of lemon on it.
  13. Enjoy!
