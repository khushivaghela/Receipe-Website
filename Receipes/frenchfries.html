FRENCH FRIES

Hi, I'm Khushi
I'm glad you are here, thankyou for joining me!

Ingredients

  • 3 Big potatoes
  • Powder: Red chilli, haldi, chaat masala, aamchur, garam-masala (can add other powder of your choice of your choice)
  • 250 ml water
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Take potatoes and peel them
  2. After peeling them, cut them in french-fries shape and let in soak in water.
  3. After a while, take out chips dry them in a cotton cloth..
  4. IN the meantime keep water on medium flame.
  5. When water start boiling, add chips for 5-7 minutes.
  6. While the chips are boiling, mix all the powder.
  7. Once the chips are 80% boiled, remove them and keep them on same cotton cloth.
  8. Keep oil on medium-high flame,fry chips till they are golden brownwhich will take 8-10 minutes
  9. Soon as you take out chips, sprinkle the masala and shake it well
  10. Enjoy
