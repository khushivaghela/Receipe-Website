COLD COFFEE WITH ICECREAM

Ingredients

  • Coffee powder - 2 tbspn
  • Sugar - 2 tbspn
  • Icecubes - 4
  • Choco powder - 1 tbspn
  • Coco powder - 1 tbspn
  • Icecream of your choice - 2-3 scoops
  • Milk - 250 ml

Steps

  1. In a blender, add all the above mentioned Ingredients
  2. Blend it.
  3. In one glass add the cold-coffee and add 1-2 scoop of icr-cream
  4. dizzle some chocolate sauce which is totally optional
  5. Enjoy
