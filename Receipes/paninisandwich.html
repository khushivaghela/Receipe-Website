About the dish

Panini sandwich is the dish you'll find at every indian wedding.

NOTE

This version is jain/Swaminarayan friendly

Ingredients
  • Italian bread (such as ciabatta and michetta), if you don't have go with your regular bread.
  • Mayonnaise - 5-6 tbspn.
  • Sauce - 2 tbspn both:
  • Red Chilli
  • Tomato
  • Powder - according to your taste
  • Red Chilli
  • Salt
  • Garam masala
  • Pepper
  • Veggies - 1 cup each
  • Cucumber
  • Tomatoes
Directions
  1. Chop veggies in a square shaped small pieces.
  2. In a bowl, add all the given ingredients and mix it well.
  3. Butter the bread & add the prepared filling in it.
  4. You can add cheese slice in between, it is totally optional.
  5. In a pre-heated greased griller, place the sandwich till the desired crisp.
  6. Serve it hot & Enjoy.
