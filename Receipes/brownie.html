BROWNIE

Hi, I'm Khushi
I'm glad you are here, thankyou for joining me!

Ingredients

  • Marie-gold biscuit - half packet
  • Sugar 14 tbspn
  • Eno - half packet
  • Choco powder - 6 tbspn
  • Coco powder - 6 tbspn
  • Milk - half cup

Steps

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients in mixer
  2. Blend till all are in fine powder
  3. Transfer the powder in the a pan and add milk
  4. Now, while you are mixing your dry and wet ingredients, take a pan and fill with half glass water, add sliced lemon and place a stand in the pan and keep it on gas
  5. After the ingredients are mixed add eno and give it a good stir.
  6. Transfer the mixture into pan and keep pan in thelarger pan already placed on stove.
  7. Let it bake for 20 minutes on medium-high flame.
  8. After the check and if tooth-pick comes clean its done or else keep it for 5 minutes and repeat till done.
  9. After the brownie's are out of pan, add a thick layer of nutella (optional) and transfer them in fridge to cool down.
  10. Enjoy
